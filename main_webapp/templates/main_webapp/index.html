FAQs

What does each charcuterie board include?

Our charcuterie boards feature a carefully curated selection of cheeses, cured meats, fresh fruits, nuts, and jams, all artfully arranged on an artisanal board.

Can I customize my board?

Yes, we offer customization options to suit your tastes and preferences. You can choose specific items or select from our suggested combinations via our customization form.

What is the delivery time?

We typically deliver within 3 to 5 business days within our service area.

Do you offer international or long-distance shipping?

No, our deliveries are strictly local and cover a radius of up to 25 miles from our location.

What is your return policy?

If your charcuterie board arrives in less than perfect condition, we offer returns or refunds within 15 days of purchase. Please refer to our returns policy for more details.
